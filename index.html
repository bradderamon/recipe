Spaghetti Pie

Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. cooked spaghetti, drained and cooled
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 1 egg lightly beaten
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 3 cups spaghetti sauce
  • 8 oz. shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning

    • Toss cooked spaghetti, cheese and egg together. Place in a 9-inch pie plate and press along bottom and side to make a crust. Press down in middle to form area for meat and sauce. Brown ground beef, drain and add garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt and pepper. Mix with spaghetti sauce. Spread mixture over spaghetti crust. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Bake at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Let cool 5 minutes before cutting into wedges.
